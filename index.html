Send Money

Send money to another Lumiere account, transfer money to bank accounts, or buy load for you or others.

Send money to another Lumiere account

  1. Login to your Lumiere account
  2. Tap "Send Money"
  3. Enter the amount to send
  4. Key in the receiver's mobile number
  5. Tap "Send"

Transfer money to bank account.

  1. Login to your Lumiere account
  2. Tap "Bank Transfer"
  3. Enter the amount to send
  4. Key in the bank account number
  5. Tap "Send"

Load to yourself or to others.

  1. Login to your Lumiere account
  2. Tap "Buy Load"
  3. Enter the amount to send
  4. Key in the phone number
  5. Tap "Send"

Pay Bills

Pay your bills through Lumiere.

  4. Type in the required information
  5. Choose the biller
  6. Confirm the payment

About Lumiere

Know more about Lumiere.

Lumiere is a free mobile e-wallet that lets you experience safe and speedy financial transactions using your mobile phone. You can install the Lumiere app on your mobile phone to buy load for any network, pay utility bills, send money, and make bank transfers wherever you are. You can cash in to your account through Lumiere's partner centers.
